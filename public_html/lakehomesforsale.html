LAKE PROPERTY FOR SALE

May 2025

If you are looking for a place on a beautiful lake where you can fish, boat, ski, kayak or just sit and watch sunrises and sunsets, this is your lake. Whether you want a weekend/summer place, full-time living or retirement, Little Swan Lake in West Central Illinois could be your answer. It's only about a 3 hour drive from the Southern part of Chicago. It's about 20 miles from Macomb, 25 miles from Galesburg, 25 miles from Monmouth and about 50 miles from Peoria. Check out the homes and building lots that are for sale. It's affordable living on a lake that lets you relax and have fun.

Little Swan Lake is served by the Abingdon-Avon CUSD #276 school district which has an elementary school and the district middle school in Avon and the high school in nearby Abingdon. For more information go to: http://www.d276.net.

It's like living on vacation!
