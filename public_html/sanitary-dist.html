Announcement: LSL Sanitary District Newly Elected officers


  • - Trustee Mat Douglass was elected President.
  • - Trustee Patrick McKinley was elected Secretary/Treasurer.
  • - Bob Breen was appointed as the newest Trustee. He will also take over duties as Water Assistant and will perform the daily water testing and be the contact person (309-335-5971) for any water issues.
  • - Larry Lawson’s contract as Water Superintendent has been renewed to 2020.
  • - Christie Butler will remain as bookkeeper.
  • - Patty Andersen will remain as recording secretary.

The State of Illinois Statute is the statue the Sanitary District was formed under and explains the powers and duties of the Sanitary District and the process for the trustees appointment.
