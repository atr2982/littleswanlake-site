LITTLE SWAN LAKE AVAILABLE JOBS

Little Swan Lake Patrol Position Open
Little Swan Lake (LSL) is located 22 miles northeast of Macomb and 4 miles west of Avon, IL
Part-time position, May through October. Hourly wage is $12-15/hour. Patrolman average 10-20 hours per week. Most patrolling is done during the highest activity times, specifically weekends and holidays, weekday hours are also required.
Qualifications:

  • ■ 21 years of age or older
  • ■ Good people skills required
  • ■ Familiar with the IL Department of Natural Resources (IDNR) rules
  • ■ Familiar with LSL club rules & regulations
  • ■ Able to work flexible schedule
  • ■ Ability to operate watercraft
  • ■ Good references
  • ■ Ability to be bonded
  • ■ Prefer LSL Member in good standing

Anyone interested in working as Lake Patrol should contact a Board Member or leave a message with the LSL Clerk (309)465-6734. Board Member contact information can be found at http://littleswanlake.net/contacts.html.
